Overview

Tactician's Crown is the pinnacle event of the set; bringing together the best players in the world with everything on the line. Players compete for the ultimate title: set champion.

Watch live with us at hhttps://www.twitch.tv/teamfighttactics

Day 1 - Round of 40
  • 40 players play 6 games, with lobbies shuffled every 2 games using snake seeding. Top 32 players advance to Round of 32 and the bottom 8 players are eliminated.
Day 2 - Round of 32
  • 32 players play 2 games. The top 24 players advance to Round of 24 and the bottom 8 players are eliminated.
Day 2 - Round of 24
  • 24 players play 2 games. The top 16 players advance to Round of 16 and the bottom 8 players are eliminated.
Day 2 - Round of 16
  • 16 players play 2 games. The top 4 players advance to the Grand Finals. The top 5th - 12th players advance to Round of 8 and the bottom 4 players are eliminated.
Day 2 - Round of 8
  • 8 players play 1 game. The top 4 players advance to the Grand Finals and the bottom 4 are eliminated.
Finals
  • The first player to reach 20 points and then win a game is crowned the winner of the Tactician’s Crown!
